About Me

Hello what’s up? Since March 2017, I am a PhD student at EPFL, under the supervision of Jérôme Scherer and Kathryn Hess. During the Spring semester 2020, I received a Doc.Mobility grant from the Swiss National Foundation and participated in the MSRI program Higher categories and categorification in Berkeley. I will be graduating in February 2021.

Research Interest

I am interested in category theory and homotopy theory. I am particularly intersted in $(\infty,n)$-categories and their models. My PhD thesis is titled Double categories and their $\infty$-analogues and can be found here.
